Marvin Library

Books installed in Marvin's library are displayed with a background color representing matches to books in your calibre library.

Color Meaning
Green This book has the same uuid as a book in your calibre library
Yellow This book exists in your calibre library, but with a different uuid
Red This book is a duplicate copy of a uuid-matched book in your calibre library
White This book exists in Marvin, but not your calibre library
